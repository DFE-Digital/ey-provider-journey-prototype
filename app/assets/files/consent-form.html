We need your consent to share your personal details

We are required to get your written consent to share your personal details with the Department for Education (DfE) to:

  • apply for an Early Years Financial Incentive Payment (recruitment bonus) on your behalf
  • comply with data protection and confidentiality regulations

How we will use your personal details

Your personal details will be used to submit a claim application for the recruitment bonus. The recruitment bonus is a £1,000 payment which is available to new and returning early years educators.

What personal details will be shared

We need your consent to provide DfE with the following personal details:

  • your full name
  • your email address
  • your employment start date
