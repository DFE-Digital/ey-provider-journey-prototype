Overview

This scheme enables you to receive a number of £1000 payments to attach to job adverts to recruit practitioners. These can be used to recruit for part and full-time roles.

The aim is to support nurseries with recruitment and increase the number of permanent practitioners in the early years sector.

How it works

Your local authority will have a set number of financial incentives that you and other nurseries in your local authority can use to recruit staff.

After you’ve recruited a new employee, you need to start a claim on their behalf on a digital service. The employee will then receive an email inviting them to complete the claim.

The employee will receive the £1000 payment after they’ve been in their role for 6 months.
