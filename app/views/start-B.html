Before you start

Use this service to make an early years financial incentive payment claim for an employee.

To make a claim for your employee, they must:

  • have applied for a role with an incentive attached
  • have started their role after 15 May 2024
  • spend at least 70% of their time working directly with children
  • not have worked in some early years roles in the six months before starting their current role

If your employee worked in early years in the 6 months before starting their current role

You can still make a claim for your employee if their previous role was:

  • temporary or fixed term, including as an apprentice or agency worker
  • permanent but didn’t involve working directly with children
  • permanent but involved working directly with children less than 70% of the time

Information you'll need to provide

You’ll need to provide some details about your employee to make a claim. You should ensure you have collected their written consent to give us this information before you start.

You’ll need their:

  • full name
  • start date
  • email address
  • National Insurance number

We’ll also ask you to confirm that they meet the eligibility criteria.

Cancelling a claim

If your employee leaves their role before they’ve been in it for 6 months, you should cancel their claim.

You can cancel a claim by contacting us at help@opsteam.education.gov.uk.
