Guidance

Before you start

Use this service to make an early years financial incentive payment claim for an employee.

To make a claim for an employee, they must:

  • have applied for a job with an incentive attached
  • have started their job after 15 May 2024
  • spend most of the time in their role, working directly with children

'Most of their time' means:

  • 70% or more of their contracted hours are spent working directly with children

'Working directly with children' could involve any of the following:

  • guiding children in their learning
  • supporting children with their learning and development
  • interacting with children
  • giving hands-on care
  • working 'in the room', in nursery settings that have 'rooms'

Other conditions for the payment

The purpose of this incentive scheme is to increase the number of people working with children in permanent roles, within the early years sector.

So to make a claim for an employee, they must also have:

  • not worked in early years in the six months before starting their current role

Or alternatively if they did work in early years in the 6 months before starting their current job, this can have been in any of the following types of employment:

  • Casual employment and temporary work including:
    • zero-hour contracts
    • guaranteed minimum hour contracts
    • self-employed or freelance contracts
    • fixed term contracts (FTCs)
  • voluntary or unpaid work
  • permanent employment, where their role involved working with children less than 70% of their time:
    • This means the main focus of their role did not involve working directly with children. Examples of this might include people who worked previously as gardeners, chefs, or administrators.

Information you'll need to provide

You’ll need to provide some details about your employee to make a claim. You will be doing this on behalf of the company or organisation the employee works for.

You should make sure you have collected their written consent to give us this information before you start. You can use our consent form to do this

You'll need their:

  • full name
  • start date
  • email address
  • PAYE reference number

We’ll also ask you to confirm that they meet the eligibility criteria.

Cancelling a claim

If your employee leaves their role before they’ve been in it for 6 months, you should cancel their claim.

You can cancel a claim by contacting us at help@opsteam.education.gov.uk.
