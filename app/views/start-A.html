Before you start

Use this service to make an early years financial incentive payment claim for an employee.

To make a claim for your employee, they must:

  • have applied for a role with an incentive attached
  • have started their role after 15 May 2024
  • spend at least 70% of their time working directly with children

They must also not have worked in early years in the six months before starting their current role, unless their previous role:

  • was temporary or fixed term
  • involved working directly with children less than 70% of the time

Information you'll need to provide

You’ll need to provide some details about your employee to make a claim. You should ensure you have collected their written consent to give us this information to us before you start.

You’ll need their:

  • full name
  • start date
  • email address
  • National Insurance number

We’ll also ask you to confirm that they meet the eligibility criteria.

Cancelling a claim

If your employee leaves their role before they’ve been in it for 6 months, you should cancel the claim. You can do that by contacting us at help@opsteam.education.gov.uk.
